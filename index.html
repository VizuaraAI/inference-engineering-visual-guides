About This Course

This course provides a complete visual and hands-on curriculum for mastering the inference stack. From the fundamental building blocks of neural networks and transformer architectures to the cutting-edge optimization techniques used in production systems, every concept is brought to life through interactive visual walkthroughs and runnable code labs.

Based on "Inference Engineering" by Philip Kiely, this curriculum covers the entire journey from understanding how a single forward pass works to deploying and scaling inference workloads across multi-cloud environments. Whether you are an ML engineer looking to deepen your understanding or a systems engineer entering the AI inference space, this course meets you where you are.

Built with care by Vizuara AI Labs — making complex AI systems understandable through visual education.
